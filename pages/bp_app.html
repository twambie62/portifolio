Blood pressure App:
connected to Arduino Esp-32 to measure blood Pressure
Health UI/UX Design App development Kotlin Android development Jetpack compose Arduino Embedded systems Electronics
This project is a remote blood pressure monitoring system designed to enable continuous patient monitoring outside the hospital environment. It combines embedded hardware, IoT communication, and a mobile/cloud platform to allow doctors to track patients' blood pressure in real time.
